Determining the correct HVAC size for a home in **Chicago, IL** requires precise engineering data. Chicago's unique climate requires careful consideration of both peak heating and cooling loads.

## Local Climate Design Data

In **Chicago**, HVAC systems must be designed to handle the extremes of both winter heating and summer cooling. According to engineering databases:

*   **Winter Design Temperature (99%):** -1°F

## Why Precision Sizing Matters in IL

In IL's unique climate, an "oversized" system is just as problematic as an undersized one. Chicago homes often suffer from:

1.  **Poor Dehumidification:** The AC doesn't run long enough to pull moisture from the air.
2.  **Shortened Lifespan:** Increased wear and tear on the compressor.
3.  **High Utility Bills:** Starting a motor consumes more energy than letting it run steadily.
