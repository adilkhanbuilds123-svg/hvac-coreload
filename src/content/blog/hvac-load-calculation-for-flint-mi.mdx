Determining the correct HVAC size for a home in **Flint, MI** requires precise engineering data. Flint's unique climate requires careful consideration of both peak heating and cooling loads.

In **Flint**, HVAC systems must be designed to handle the extremes of both winter heating and summer cooling. According to engineering databases:

## Why Precision Sizing Matters in MI

In MI's unique climate, an "oversized" system is just as problematic as an undersized one. Flint homes often suffer from:

1.  **Poor Dehumidification:** The AC doesn't run long enough to pull moisture from the air.
2.  **Shortened Lifespan:** Increased wear and tear on the compressor.
3.  **High Utility Bills:** Starting a motor consumes more energy than letting it run steadily.
